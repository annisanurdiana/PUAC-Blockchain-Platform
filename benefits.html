Key Benefits

Tracking and Automating the Supply Chain

Data is gathered and time-stamped at each step along the supply chain as each shipment is picked up and delivered. As each transaction occurs, all participants are kept up to date in real time. PUAC boosts efficiency, increases visibility, maintains stock, automates freight orders, and reduces human paperwork through this method.

Visibility of Data and Information

By making commodities stockpiles visible, consumers can see how much goods they own, where it is going, when it will arrive, and how much it is worth. Users of PUAC have the ability to make judgments based on reliable information and market data. Users can be certain that they have the information they need to maximize their bottom line at every point of the supply chain

Smart Contracting

Buyers, sellers, and freighters of commodities will be able to interact in a blockchain-backed environment, ensuring that both sides of the transaction are protected. The goal of PUAC is to provide the most advanced automated software for creating commodity and freight contracts. PUAC will use blockchain technology to construct Indonesia's first worldwide marketplace for purchasing and selling tangible agricultural goods.

Keeping Records and Providing Proof of Origin

Proof of origin may be established as commodities move through the supply chain by maintaining an immutable blockchain record of their trip. At each level, key supply chain players and supply chain nodes owned and managed by these businesses (such as fields, silos, vehicles, and delivery sites) are documented. PUAC also keeps track of important commodity information such weights, kinds, variations, grades, specifications, and inputs.

Financial Benefits

Reducing the Cost of Capital

By offering considerably improved visibility over farm inventory and decreasing financiers risk exposure, PUAC aims to make financing against stock on hand more accessible and affordable for farmers. With PUAC, banks can view the farmer's current 'position,' as well as a history of custody, all of which is recorded and certified by the blockchain. In addition, any payment guarantees (future sales guaranteed by a smart contract) that the farmer has committed with are visible to financiers.

Improving Cash Flow

Agricultural supply chain participants are impacted by obstructed financial flows in their day-to-day activities. Businesses try to match receivables to payables to create a predictable and consistent cash flow. Unfortunately, the amount of outstanding debt grows as agricultural goods move through the supply chain. PUAC helps supply chain partners improve their cash flow by allowing them to fund their operations via forward (blockchain-backed) contracts with current customers.

Insurance

Insurance businesses may more efficiently evaluate claims, offer personalized products to consumers, disburse payments on claims, manage risk, identify suspicious behavior, and enhance fraud assessment by obtaining access to live, accurate, and unchangeable data. Farmers, purchasers, and logistics businesses will be able to choose from a larger selection of insurance policies with more realistic risk premiums and lower costs in exchange.

Peer-to-Peer Lending

The PUAC platform allows like-minded farmers to come to an agreement on financing terms that go beyond standard overdraft loans, lowering the cost of agricultural financial products. Farms of all sizes will be able to connect through PUAC in a secure peer-to-peer (P2P) lending environment, allowing firms with cash surpluses to give short-term loans to those in need.
