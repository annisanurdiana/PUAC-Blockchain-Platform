The Goal of PUAC

Agricultural supply chains are the backbone of many cultures and are necessary for communities to thrive. PUAC is to become the greatest industry-wide platform for managing the agricultural supply chain, combining sellers and buyers, and helping bulk logistics businesses to manage and develop their operations.
